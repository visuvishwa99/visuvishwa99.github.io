About Me

As a seasoned Senior Data Engineer, I’m Vishwa.P, bringing over a decade of experience in architecting scalable, cloud-native data platforms that power intelligent automation, real-time analytics, and strategic decision-making across finance, banking, retail, telecom, and food-tech industries. I specialize in building robust ETL pipelines and Medallion architecture frameworks using tools like AWS (EMR, Glue, Redshift), Azure (ADF), and Databricks—seamlessly integrating diverse data sources into secure, unified lakehouse solutions.

My strategic edge lies in integrating AI-powered automation and real-time orchestration into the data lifecycle leveraging technologies like Spark, Kafka, and dbt to build intelligent, self-optimizing data pipelines. I consistently deliver high-impact solutions that blend domain knowledge with cutting-edge cloud and AI engineering—ensuring scalability, reliability, and business alignment at every step.
