About Me

Hi, I'm Vishwa.P—a Senior Data Engineer who's spent the last 10+ years turning messy data into powerful business insights. I love building data platforms that actually work in the real world, helping companies in finance, banking, retail, telecom, and food-tech make smarter decisions faster.

What gets me excited? Taking fragmented data from all over the place and creating clean, reliable pipelines that teams can actually depend on. I work with tools like AWS, Azure, and Databricks to build what I call "data ecosystems"—secure, scalable solutions where your data can breathe and grow with your business.

Here's where it gets interesting: I don't just move data around—I make it intelligent. By integrating AI automation and real-time processing with technologies like Spark, Kafka, and dbt, I create pipelines that learn and adapt. Think of them as data systems that get smarter over time, reducing manual work while delivering exactly what your business needs.
